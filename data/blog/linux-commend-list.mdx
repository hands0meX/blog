- 实时日志
```sh
tail -F xxx.txt
```

- 开启脚本进程
```sh
nohup python your_script.py >> output.log 2>&1 &
```

- 结束脚本进程
```sh
pid=$(ps aux | grep "nohup your_command" | grep -v grep | awk '{print $2}')
kill -9 $pid
```
